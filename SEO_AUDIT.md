## Suggested SEO Content Blocks For The Instagram Page

### 1. Hero

Use a title that includes the main keyword but also states the value:

`Free Instagram QR Code Generator - Create Custom QR Codes for Profile, Reels and Posts`

Hero copy should mention:

- Free generation
- No sign-up for static QR
- Custom colors/logo
- PNG/SVG/WebP downloads
- Works for Instagram profile, post, Reel, and highlight links

### 2. How-To Steps

Add a concise step section:

1. Enter your Instagram username or paste your Instagram URL.
2. Choose profile, post, Reel, or custom URL destination.
3. Customize the QR design with brand colors, logo, frame, and CTA.
4. Test the QR code on iPhone and Android.
5. Download as PNG for digital use or SVG for print.

Mark this up with `HowTo` schema.

### 3. Instagram Native QR vs QR Junction

Add a comparison section covering:

- Profile only vs profile/posts/Reels/highlights
- Basic Instagram styling vs custom brand styling
- JPEG screenshot vs PNG/SVG/WebP export
- No analytics vs analytics if you support dynamic links
- Static destination vs editable dynamic destination if supported
- Casual use vs business marketing use

### 4. Print Guide

Add practical sizing guidance:

- Business card: minimum 2 x 2 cm, SVG preferred
- Flyer: 3-5 cm depending on scan distance
- Poster: use the 10:1 distance rule
- Storefront: larger QR with high contrast and short CTA
- Packaging sticker: test on curved or glossy surfaces

Also explain:

- Keep quiet zone around the code
- Avoid low contrast gradients
- Do not cover too much of the center with logo
- Always test before bulk printing

### 5. India-Specific Use Cases

Examples to add:

- Cafe table tents: “Scan to follow our Instagram for daily specials”

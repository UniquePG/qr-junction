2. Problem Statement

Traditional QR generators only create images.

Businesses cannot:

- Know who scanned
- Know where scans came from
- Track campaign performance
- Update destinations after printing
- Measure ROI
- Collect leads
- Track conversions

This creates a huge gap between offline marketing and online analytics.

QR Junction solves this gap.

Feature 1

Dynamic QR Codes

Problem

Static QR codes cannot be edited after printing.

Solution

QR code points to:

https://qrjunction.in/q/abc123

instead of:

https://destination.com

Flow

User scans QR

↓

QR Junction receives request

↓

Analytics stored

↓

User redirected

↓

Destination opens

Benefits

- Editable anytime
- Trackable
- Campaign friendly
- Long-term usable

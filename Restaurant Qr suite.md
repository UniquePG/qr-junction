Restaurant Qr Suite

Product Vision
Enable restaurants, cafes, food courts, cloud kitchens and hotels to create, manage and optimize their digital menu experience through QR codes menu performance analytics.


Phase 1 — MVP (Launch Fast)
Goal:
Allow restaurants to upload menus, generate QR codes and display beautiful digital menus.
